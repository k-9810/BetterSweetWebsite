Simple Way to Track Daily Sugar Intake

What is BetterSweet?

BetterSweet is an app designed for diabetic patients, to assist them in tracking their daily sugar intake. With our app, you can take a picture directly of your food or the food label to automatically log the sugar intake from each meal you have.

Our App's Main Functions:

  • Take a picture of your food or a food label and/or upload an existing photo to automatically log food.
  • When food is logged, your daily sugar intake is updated, to demonstrate how much sugar you have had so far.
  • This app will also keep track of the food you have consumed and logged in the app for future reference.
